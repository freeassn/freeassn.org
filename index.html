In the internet age, email has become one of the most critically important services that any person uses. Unfortunately, many people have seemingly become locked into proprietary email providers that do not respect their privacy. Free Association has set out to offer a viable alternative to these providers for those who seek to protect their privacy and return control over their digital lives to themselves.

Free Association provides low-cost, privacy respecting hosted email on a fully free-and-open-source software stack. Currently, we are the primary email provider for about 100 individuals. As of October, 2024 we are in the process of structuring Free Association as a worker-owned cooperative.
